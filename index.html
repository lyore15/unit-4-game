Crystal Collector


Here's how you play the game

  1. You given a random number at the start of the game.
  2. There are four crystals below
    • Blue
    • Yellow
    • Green
    • Red
  3. By clicking the crystal, you will addd points to your score.
  4. You win the game by matching your score to the random number.
  5. You lose if your total score goes above the random number
  6. The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it.
  7. Each time a new game starts, the value of the crystal will change.
